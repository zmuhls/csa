The nominations for second trustee to serve the other three year term were as follows;

Mrs.L. Miller nominated Chas.E. Lockrow, seconded by Mrs.Rice.
Thos.Kerton nominated H.McClellan, seconded by C.Getford. Mrs.H.I. Grounds nominated Gerald Grounds, seconded by Mrs.Lansley. Mrs. Helmuth nominated F.Gilmore, seconded by Mrs.Reinick, Mr.Gilmore declined. Mrs.McNay nominated Mrs.Rice, seconded by Mrs.L.Miller. Mrs.Rice declined. Nominations closed with three men in the field, C.E.Lockrow, H.McClellan and Gerald Grounds. There were 293 votes cast, 160 for H.McClellan, 93 for C.E.Lockrow and 37 for Gerald Grounds/which result put Mr.McClellan in office as trustee for a three (3) year term.

The nominations for first trustee to serve a two year term were as follows;

Mrs.Bishop nominated Grover Long, seconded by Mrs.Hunter.
Mrs.LeGrange nominated A.Hagey, seconded by Mrs.Genson, Mr.Hagey declined. John Dott nominated Al.Carter, seconded by Fred Dott. Mrs.Helmuth nominated Wm.Loechel, seconded by Wm.Reinick. Thos. Kerton nominated by Thos.Rose, seconded by Mrs.Ruduskey. Mr. Kerton declined. R.E.Grounds nominated F.Gilmore, seconded by E. Bellamy. John Dott nominated John Fisher, seconded by Mrs.Merkle. Mr.Fisher declined. The nominations were closed with four men in the field, Grover Long, Al.Carter, F.Gilmore and Wm.Loechel. There were 244 votes cast, 131 for Long, 52 for Carter, 38 for Gilmore and 23 for Loechel/which result put Mr.Long in office as trustee for a two (2) year term.
