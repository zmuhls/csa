E.N. Pratt received 15,E,W. Neff received 7 and Frank Lewis received 6.
The chairman declared Messrs Arthur Stevens and E.M. Pratt duly elected as members of Board of Education to serve until August I,1916.
Frank Lewis and E.W. Neff nominated and duly elected as members of Board of Education to serve until August I,1917, by the clerk casting one ballot for each according to motion duly carried.
Dr. C.S. Butler nominated and duly elected as member of Board of Education to serve until August I,1918, the clerk casting one ballot according to motion duly carried.
