NEW YORK STATE TEACHERS' ASSOCIATION.

National Summer School
GLENS FALLS, N.Y.

Beginning July 20th, 1897.

CIRCULARS will be sent free to any one on application. They will also be found at the Meeting of the State Teachers' Association. Excursion fare from New York to Glens Falls and return will be $8.88. The same for Niagara Falls or Buffalo, $13.85.

The work of this school embraces a Department of Professional Training with such instructors as Dr. Richard G. Aldrich, Dr. Edgar C. Boone, Dubbs Shimer, Supt. George I. Penmanship, Drawing, Elementary Science, Manual Training, Physical Training, Kindergarten Work, Study of Shakespeare.

The only Summer School in the state devoted exclusively to the professional needs of teachers. This is one of the few thoroughly equipped and adequately administered American Summer Schools.

For further information address,
SHERMAN WILLIAMS,
Manager,
GLENS FALLS, N.Y.

[stamp: New York State Teachers' Association]

Viewed by the
Secretary.
A.
Dr. Thomas
Park Secretary.
