New York State Teachers
DISTRICT OFFICERS AND MEETINGS

NORTHERN DISTRICT
Franklin and St. Lawrence Counties
Meeting Place, Potsdam, Thursday and Friday, October 6-7, 1927

OFFICERS
President, Gertrude E. Hyde, District Superintendent of Schools, Moira; Vice-President, Arthur J. Laidlaw, Superintendent of Schools, Ogdensburg; Secretary-Treasurer, Charles Van Housen, Principal Senior High School, Potsdam Normal School.

CENTRAL DISTRICT
Cayuga, Hamilton, Herkimer, Jefferson, Lewis, Madison, Oneida, Onondaga, Oswego and Seneca Counties
Meeting Place, Syracuse, Thursday and Friday, October 27-28, 1927

OFFICERS
President, George R. Bodley, Superintendent of Schools, Fulton; Vice-President, George R. Staley, Superintendent of Schools, Rome; Secretary-Treasurer, Mabel A. DeVinney, School Principal, Syracuse.

EASTERN DISTRICT
Albany, Clinton, Columbia, Dutchess, Essex, Fulton, Greene, Montgomery, Rensselaer, Saratoga, Schenectady, Schoharie, Ulster, Warren and Washington Counties
Meeting Place, Troy, Thursday and Friday, October 27-28, 1927

OFFICERS
President, Charles L. Mosher, Superintendent of Schools, Saratoga Springs; Vice-President, Eta McTammany, High School, Troy; Secretary-Treasurer, Evan E. Jones, Superintendent of Schools, Mechanicville.

CENTRAL WESTERN DISTRICT
Allegany, Livingston, Monroe, Ontario, Orleans, part of Steuben, Wayne, Wyoming and Yates Counties
Meeting Place, Rochester, Thursday and Friday, November 3-4, 1927

OFFICERS
President, F. Neff Stroup, Superintendent of Schools, Newark; Vice-President, Mabel Simpson, Rochester; Secretary-Treasurer, W. W. Rayfield, District Superintendent of Schools, Webster.

[stamp: New York State Teachers Association]
