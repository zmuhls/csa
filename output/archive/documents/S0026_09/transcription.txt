Excursion Route

ON FRIDAY, JULY 20, 1897
at 12 M. the excursion party will leave Normal College walk one block to 3d Ave. and board the special train in waiting at the 6th Street station. The boat will leave Pier (New) No. 1, North River, at 1 P.M. The trip will include a sail up the Hudson River to Grant's Tomb, passing various points of interest such as Castle Garden, Staten Island, the Narrows, Coney Island, etc., returning to the city by the way of the East River, including a good view of the East Bridge, Brooklyn. The boat will land at 335 St., East River not later than 6 P.M. On leaving the city there are several cars which run north to 34th Street. One block north, at the foot of 34th Street, there is a hotel section of the city. On running the several blocks of the hotel section of the city, together with coffee, a box luncheon, tea will be served.
