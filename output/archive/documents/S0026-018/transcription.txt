**THE NEW YORK SOCIETY OF PEDAGOGY**

* * *

**THE LIBRARY AND READING ROOM**

The members are at Grammar School No. 6, 85th Street and Madison Avenue. Here members will find an excellent pedagogical library of twelve hundred volumes, as well as numerous books of reference and the more important magazines, which are the later educational publications. These books may be taken out by the members or may be consulted in quiet and comfort in the reading room, which is open on school days from 8 A.M. to 6 P.M. and on Saturdays from 9 A.M. to 6 P.M. Miss Hawthorne, the librarian, being in attendance during these hours.

* * *

**Classes are given weekly.**

Classes are given in subjects connected with school work.
