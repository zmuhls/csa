The undersigned inhabitants of School District No. 1, in the town of Wellsville, entitled to vote at any meeting of the Inhabitants of said district, hereby call for a meeting to be held for the purpose of determining by a vote of such district whether a Union Free School shall be established therein, in conformity to the provisions of chapter 433 of the Laws of 1853.

Dated Wellsville, October 12, 1859.
